About CAFE NOVA

Our Passion Story

Founded in 2010 in Colombo by passionate coffee enthusiast Danushka Wickramasinghe, CAFE NOVA started as a small family-run café with a bold mission: to bring world-class coffee culture to Sri Lanka while supporting local farmers and fostering genuine connections within our community.

What began in a converted home kitchen has grown into a beloved gathering place for coffee lovers across the island. Despite our growth, we remain committed to our roots—roasting our beans in small batches using traditional methods, and sourcing high-quality Arabica and Robusta beans from the misty hills of Nuwara Eliya, Kandy, and Ella, as well as trusted fair-trade partners in Ethiopia, Colombia, and beyond.

Our team of baristas are trained in both the science and artistry of coffee—from perfect extraction techniques to intricate latte art—ensuring that every cup at CAFE NOVA is crafted with precision, care, and Sri Lankan warmth.

We believe coffee is more than just a drink. It's a moment of calm, a reason to meet, and a celebration of culture. Whether you're working on your next big idea, catching up with friends, preparing for exams, or simply relaxing, CAFE NOVA is your home away from home.
